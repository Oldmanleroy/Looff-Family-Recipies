Artichoke Dip

This is the Looff family recipe for Artichoke Dip!

You'll need:

  • 1 Large Jar of Marinated Artichokes
  • 1/2 Cup of Mayo (Bonus Points if you make it yourself)
  • 1/4 Cup of Diced Onions
  • Paprika to your Taste!

Intrustions:

Pull leaves off of the artichoke hearts, and use the hearts only (I know, morbid). Rinse and dice. Mix with the other ingredients and put in a casserole dish. Sprinkle that paprika in, then bake at 375 for 20 minutes. Serve with your vehicle of choice!
