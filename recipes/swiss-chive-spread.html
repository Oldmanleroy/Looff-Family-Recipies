Swiss Chive Spread

This is the Looff family recipe for Swiss Chive Spread!

You'll need:

  • 3 Cups (12 Ounces) of Finely Shredded Swiss Cheese
  • 3/4 Cup of Mayo (Bonus Points if you make it yourself)
  • 1/4 cup of Chopped Chives
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Pepper

Intructions:

  1. Combine cheese and mayo in a bowl.
  2. Add chives, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir well, cover, and chill!
  4. Serve with buttery crackers :3
